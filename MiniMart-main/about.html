Our Mission

MiniMart was created to solve a real problem faced by college students everywhere - the need for an affordable, trustworthy marketplace to buy and sell used items.

As students ourselves, we understand the financial challenges of college life. Textbooks are expensive, electronics break, and dorm space is limited. That's why we built MiniMart - to create a community where students can help each other by trading items they no longer need.
